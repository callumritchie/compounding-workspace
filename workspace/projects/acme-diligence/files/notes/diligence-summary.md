# Acme — Acquisition Diligence (COMPLETED)

**Client:** Acme Health · **Engagement:** commercial diligence on a bolt-on RPM (remote patient monitoring) target · **Status:** complete.

## What we were asked
Assess whether the target's remote-monitoring book was durable enough to justify the multiple, and where the integration risk sat.

## Headline conclusion
The revenue was real but **reimbursement-dependent** — a large share sat on RPM/CCM codes exposed to threshold tightening. We recommended proceeding at a lower multiple with an earn-out tied to retained monitored-patient volume.

## How the decision actually got made
The deal only moved once Acme's CFO was convinced the downside case held. Once finance signed off, the rest of Acme's leadership committed quickly. The workshops and steering meetings were confirmation theatre; the real gate was the CFO's finance review.
