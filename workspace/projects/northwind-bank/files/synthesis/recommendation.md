# Recommendation — Northwind digital lending

Expand in two phases, gated on a **stress-tested downside**: the CFO and credit committee will not approve a growth case that only works in benign conditions.

The binding constraint is **finance's risk appetite**, not product readiness — the same pattern we see with hospital-network finance sponsors. Lead the board case with the downside (loss-rate shock, funding-cost spike), then the upside. An upside-only case stalls.
