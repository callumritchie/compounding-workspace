# Objectives — Acme Health "Growth strategy"

The north star for this engagement, distilled from the signed statement of work.
Everything we do should serve one of these; if a workstream doesn't, we should be
able to say why.

- Deliver a board-ready recommendation on Acme Health's transition to value-based care
- Build the CFO's financial sensitivity / downside case so the recommendation survives scrutiny
- Secure CFO and COO alignment on the recommended direction ahead of the board readout
- Stay within the fixed-fee scope: strategy and financial case, not implementation, vendor selection, or the clinical operating model
