# Interview — CFO, Acme Health

**Date:** 2026-06-18 · **Interviewer:** Callum

## Summary
The CFO is open to value-based care but deeply sceptical of optimistic modelling.
Her decision rule is explicit and non-negotiable.

## Key quotes
- "I don't sign off on a single-number forecast. Show me the range, and show me
  what happens when the assumptions break. **If there's no sensitivity analysis,
  the answer is no.**"
- "Operations always tells me the transition is smooth. My job is to ask what
  happens in the year it isn't."
- "I've killed three business cases in two years — every one of them because the
  downside wasn't credible."

## Interpretation
- Willingness to pay for the change exists, but is gated on financial rigour.
- The real buying criterion is *credible downside protection*, not upside size.
- Recommendation must lead with a sensitivity/stress case, not the base case.
