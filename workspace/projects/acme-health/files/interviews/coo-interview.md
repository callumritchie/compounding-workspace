# Interview — COO, Acme Health

**Date:** 2026-06-20 · **Interviewer:** Alice

## Summary
The COO is the strongest internal advocate for value-based care and is confident
about operational feasibility — possibly over-confident.

## Key quotes
- "Clinically we're ready. Care coordination is already 80% of the way there."
- "The bottleneck isn't the model, it's finance's appetite for risk."
- "If we wait for a perfect forecast we'll never move. I'd rather pilot in two
  sites and learn."

## Interpretation
- Provides the upside/feasibility narrative; light on downside quantification.
- Tension with CFO is the central dynamic of this engagement.
- A two-site pilot could be the bridge between finance caution and ops ambition.
