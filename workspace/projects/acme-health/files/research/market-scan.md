# Market Scan — Value-Based Care Transitions

## Peer evidence
- Networks that piloted before full commitment retained margin better in year 1.
- The most common failure mode is under-modelling patient-volume shifts.
- Payer contracts vary widely; outcome definitions are the negotiation crux.

## Financial pattern
- Base cases routinely show +2–4% margin over 3 years.
- Downside cases (volume shift, coordination cost overrun) can swing to −3%.
- The spread between base and downside is where boards actually make decisions.

## Sector note (healthcare)
- Finance sponsors in hospital networks consistently require stress-tested cases
  before approving strategic shifts. Upside-only cases stall.
