# Acme Health — Project Kickoff Notes

**Client:** Acme Health (regional hospital network, 12 sites)
**Engagement:** Assess a proposed shift to a value-based care contracting model.
**Sponsor:** CFO (economic buyer). COO is the day-to-day stakeholder.

## Context
- Acme is under margin pressure; payers are pushing outcome-based contracts.
- Leadership is split: finance is cautious, operations is enthusiastic.
- Board wants a recommendation in 8 weeks with a clear financial case.

## Working questions
1. Is value-based care net-positive for Acme's margin over 3 years?
2. What operational changes are required, and are they feasible?
3. What is the downside if patient volumes shift as modelled?

## Norms observed so far
- The CFO will not accept a recommendation without a downside/sensitivity case.
- Decisions are made in the Thursday finance review, not in workshops.
