# Engagement brief — Acme care-model expansion

**Status:** kick-off · **Client:** Acme Health · **Sector:** healthcare

Acme wants to extend its value-based care model into two new regions. We've just
been staffed; there are no interviews or analysis in this project yet — this is
day one.

The point of this (nearly empty) project is to show that even on **day one** the
agent isn't starting from scratch: it already carries the firm's policy, the
healthcare sector playbook, everything learned on the Acme account, and what we
know about Acme's CFO — all inherited through shared memory.
