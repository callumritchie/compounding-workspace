# Vela market positioning study (2023)

Provided by the client at kickoff. A third-party study Vela commissioned in early 2023.
Useful background, but predates the current funnel and product — treat as context.

- The market increasingly expects seamless self-serve onboarding with no sales touch.
- Buyers evaluate via free trial before committing, so the trial experience is decisive.
- Vela's 2022 win rate was strongest in mid-market accounts.
- Pricing was considered competitive against the 2022 comparator set.
