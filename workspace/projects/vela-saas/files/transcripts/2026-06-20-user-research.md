# User research — trial onboarding sessions (6 users)

**User 2:** I signed up, hit the dashboard, and had no idea what to do first. I closed the tab and never went back.
**User 4:** It wanted me to connect a data source before I could see anything useful. That's a big ask five minutes in — I didn't have credentials handy.
**User 5:** Honestly the product seemed fine once a colleague set it up for me. Getting to that point on my own was the hard part.
**User 6:** Pricing was never my issue — I never got far enough to care about pricing.
