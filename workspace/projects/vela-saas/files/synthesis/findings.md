# Synthesis — Vela growth diagnostic (interim findings)

Our distilled findings from the analytics review, user research, and stakeholder
interviews. These are our own analysis, produced this engagement.

- Onboarding friction in the first session is the primary driver of the trial-to-paid conversion decline.
- Users who don't reach the activation "aha" moment by day 7 almost never convert to paid.
- Most self-serve users drop off before connecting their first data source — the setup step is the wall.
- Expansion revenue is healthy, so the problem is top-of-funnel activation, not product value for those who stick.
