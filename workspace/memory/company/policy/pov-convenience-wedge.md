FIRM POV (cross-sector): convenience is a wedge, not a moat. Willingness-to-pay for convenience is real but fragile — it erodes when a lower-friction competitor appears. Never build a margin case on a convenience premium holding; use convenience to acquire, and base durable margin on cost-to-serve discipline. This pattern recurs across healthcare (RPM), payments, and retail.
