FIRM POV (healthcare): reimbursement and regulatory contraction is typically the single largest downside in a value-based-care or remote-monitoring case, and it is routinely under-modelled. Always stress-test a reimbursement-contraction sensitivity; a program that only pencils out at current reimbursement is fragile. Lead the case with this downside.
