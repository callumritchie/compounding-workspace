# What we sell

The offerings the firm is currently set up to deliver and price. The whitespace
engine diffs recurring client demand against this list — anything clients keep
asking for that is **not** here is a candidate new service line.

## Strategy
- **Growth strategy** — market entry, expansion, portfolio choices.
- **Corporate & business-unit strategy** — where to play / how to win.
- **Commercial due diligence** — buy-side / sell-side deal support.

## Analytics & research
- **Market sizing & landscape** — TAM/SAM, competitor mapping.
- **Financial modelling & sensitivity analysis** — base/upside/downside cases.
- **Customer & voice-of-market research**.

## Operations advisory (analysis only)
- **Operating-model design (target-state, on paper)** — we recommend the model.
- **Cost & margin diagnostics**.

---

## Explicitly out of scope today (we advise, we do not deliver)
- Implementation / execution support after the recommendation lands.
- Change management & organisational adoption.
- Post-merger integration delivery.
- Interim / embedded operating leadership.

> These exclusions are the point: if clients keep asking for them, that is demand
> we are turning away, not a policy to defend.
